Simple build instructions:
------------------------------------------------------------------------

Ensure you have PyInstaller installed
Ensure you have 32-bit Python 2.7 installed

Delete build/ & dist/

Run:
 - pyinstaller -p libs -i ConnectorIcon.ico --version-file=version.py --onefile Livewire-CLI.py 

 - "C:\Program Files\Microsoft SDKs\Windows\v7.1\Bin\signtool.exe" sign /tr http://timestamp.digicert.com /td sha256 /fd sha256 /f SigningCert.pfx /p "------PASSWORD------" Livewire-CLI.exe

Distribute the EXE as needed
